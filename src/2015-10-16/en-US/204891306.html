SECTION:Founder dating

ARTICLE:Where can I find a co-founder?

The best way to meet a co-founder is to go to their watering holes.  People who want to start companies:

  • Will take entrepreneurship classes to learn skills
  • Will attend area meetups to get involved in the local entrepreneurship landscape
  • Will take part in various startup competitions

Rob Go and Jay Acunzo's Boston Tech Guide is a great place to find out what's going on in the greater Boston area.
