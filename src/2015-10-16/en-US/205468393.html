What is the recommended legal reading for entrepreneurs ?

What are some good reads on legal matters?

Following is a list of readings to get you started learning about legal matters in a startup.

  • Wilmer Hale / Dealing with Lawyers (restricted use license) via Jim Baum (contact eir@mit.edu for a copy)
  • Founders’ Dilemma by Noam Wasserman - pay special attention to Chapter 6  
  • Blog post: How should startup founders split equity? 
  • The Hard thing about Hard Things by Ben Horowitz   
  • Free legal templates/boilerplates by Goodwin Procter
