How do I network effectively?


CATEGORY: General questions
SECTION: Getting started

Founder Institute has a great article that provides practical tips on how to network effectively. It outlines 9 steps to effective networking:

  1. Start with a strategy.
  2. Get out there.
  3. Be present: Quality over quantity.
  4. Give, give, give, ask.
  5. Seek leadership opportunities.
  6. Foster partnerships
  7. Be patient.
  8. Use social media.
  9. Find a "cheerleader".
