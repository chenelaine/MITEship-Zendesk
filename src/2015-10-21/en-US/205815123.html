What are some MIT student startups that raised venture capital in 2015?


CATEGORY: MIT Specific questions
SECTION: Startups founded by MIT alumni

Please note that this is just a sample of student startups that raised capital in 2015 - it is not meant to be exhaustive.

Finsix, a provider of small and light laptop chargers co-founded by MIT alumni Vanessa Green and Anthony Sagneri, recently completed a $7M Series B round in March 2015.

Ministry of Supply, a provider of technical businesswear for men - co-founded by Kevin Rustagi and MIT alumni Gihan Amarasiriwardena, Kit Hickey and Aman Advani in 2012, recently completed a $1.02M venture round in June 2015.

Pillpack, an online pharmacy startup co-founded by TJ Parker and MIT Alumni Elliot Cohen and Brian Hoffer in 2013, recently completed a $50M Series C financing in June 2015.

Ecovent, a company providing an intelligent home climate zoning system co-founded by MIT alumni Dip Patel and Yoel Kelman in 2012, recently completed a $6.9M Series A financing round in July 2015.
