Technology and business enablement: Microsoft for Startups


CATEGORY: Free or discounted services for startups
SECTION: Business systems


Entrepreneurs are welcome to apply for the Microsoft for Startups Program (which has replaced the previous BizSpark Program)

Entrepreneurs can get $200 per month of FREE Azure cloud services. Additional benefits may be available for MIT affiliated startups who are working with the MIT Venture Mentoring Service (VMS) (http://vms.mit.edu) based on needs and usage upon contact with VMS Microsoft liaison). 

Check out perks and eligibility here. 

Qualifying startups meet the following criteria:

  • Offer an innovative, technical solution that supports customers in their digital transformation
  • Less than five years in existence
  • Less than $10M in total annual revenue
  • Seed or Series A, or equivalent phase of development
  • Up to $20M in funding

To learn more about the application process, reach out to startups@microsoft.com
