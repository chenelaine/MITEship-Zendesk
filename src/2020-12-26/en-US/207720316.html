Should I classify my staff as employees or subcontractors?


CATEGORY: Legal
SECTION: Employee or Subcontractor?

The employee-versus-subcontractor issue is a complex question without a clear answer in many cases.  The best path forward is to share your specific situation with an employment lawyer and get their opinions on the pro's and con's of each approach.  

Morse, Barnes and Pendleton has a great article on this topic - feel free to read it but you really should work with a lawyer to make a final decision.
