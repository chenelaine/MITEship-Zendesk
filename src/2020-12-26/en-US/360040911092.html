What can companies do to cut costs during a global crisis?


CATEGORY: Anti-Fragility in the face of crisis
SECTION: Starting and running a business in tough times

In a March 2020 article, Inc.com offers four ways a company can cut cost during a global crisis.

1. Stop Hiring to Fill Open Positions

2. Compensate people with equity, not cash. 

3. Renegotiate rent.

4. Negotiate other expenses.
