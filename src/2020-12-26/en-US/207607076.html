What are some good readings on hardware and manufacturing?


CATEGORY: Product Development
SECTION: Hardware Development

Note: These resources are provided for your information only. Trust Center does not endorse nor holds an interest in any of these organizations.

  • Ben Einstein, Managing Director of Bolt VC, a hardware foused fund based in Boston and San Francisco, writes an excellent blog on all things hardware.
  • Dragon Innovation, a manufacturing consultancy based in Boston, maintains a very good blog on hardware development and manufacturing with an offshoring focus.  Scott Miller, the Founder and CEO of Dragon Innovation and a partner at Bolt, also has open sourced his Design For Manufacturing class. Dragon also provides a free BOM template called the Dragon Standard BOM for first time hardwarians.  Lastly they also have some good stuff on Slideshare. 
  • HAX (f.k.a. HAXL8R), a San Francisco based hardware accelerator, has published several decks to summarize their take on the state of hardware, robotics and more, including: Hardware Review - 1Q 2018 Trends and outlook, Building hardware startups from zero to IPO by Ben Joffe and more.
