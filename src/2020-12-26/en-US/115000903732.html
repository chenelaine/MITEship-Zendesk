Which accelerators don't take equity?


CATEGORY: Raising money - Grants, prizes and accelerators
SECTION: Accelerators

Most college based accelerators don't take equity - MIT's delta , Stanford's Start X and McGill's X1-Accelerator are examples.

In addition there are also several accelerators run by non profits or corporations that don't take equity. Examples include 

  • MassChallenge 
  • LightHouse Labs
  • IMPACT

An undated Startup Freak article lists a number of equity free accelerators all over the world.

https://startupfreak.com/10-programs-around-the-world-giving-equity-free-money-to-startups/

New programs come up all the time, including accelerators that are backed by government organizations (including many international options), non profits, for-profit corporations, venture capital firms. We suggest you do a web search on "equity free startup accelerators" to find updated lists of accelerators that may meet your needs.
