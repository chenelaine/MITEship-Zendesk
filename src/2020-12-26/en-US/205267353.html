How do I file papers for incorporation?


CATEGORY: Legal
SECTION: Company formation / incorporation

You can file your own incorporation papers with the SEC of your state.  That is not the most straightforward path.

The easiest thing to do is to hire a corporate attorney to do it for you. There are many lawyers in the Boston area with startup practices that can help you with this task.

If you are an MIT student, you can request a meeting with the Boston University Law Clinic for a consultation.
