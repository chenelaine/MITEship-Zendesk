How do I create a career plan?


CATEGORY: Career Choice
SECTION: Planning your career

MIT Global Education and Career Development has a great article that offers these 8 steps to an effective career plan:

  • Identify Your Career Options.  
  • Prioritize.      
  • Make Comparisons.
  • Consider Other Factors.      
  • Make a Choice.      
  • Set "SMART" Goals.
  • Create Your Career Action Plan.
  • Meet with a Career Adviser.
