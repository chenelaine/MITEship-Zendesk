Which incubators and accelerators focus on food tech?


CATEGORY: Raising money - Grants, prizes and accelerators
SECTION: Accelerators

In recent years there has been an explosion of food startup incubators and accelerators. A few examples are listed as follows.

  • Chobani incubator https://chobaniincubator.com/
  • Terra - Food and Ag Tech Accelerator https://www.terraaccelerator.com/ 
  • Techstars Farm to Fork - Techstars food and ag tech accelerator backed by Farm to Fork  https://www.techstars.com/farm-to-fork-program/ 
  • Food-X - Food innovation accelerator https://food-x.com/
  • Plug and Play Tech Center - Food and beverage https://www.plugandplaytechcenter.com/food/
  • Food Future Co https://www.foodfuture.co/
  • Good Food Accelerator https://familyfarmed.org/good-food-accelerator/
  • ...

 

This list was compiled with a quick web search in February 2020. New accelerators pop up all the time - be sure to do a web search with the keywords "Food startup accelerator" or "Food and ag tech accelerator" to find the latest active incubators and accelerators in this space.
