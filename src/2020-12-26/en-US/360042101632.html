Where can I find e-learning resources for Disciplined Entrepreneurship?


CATEGORY: General questions
SECTION: Startup / Entrepreneurship Frameworks

First, the Disciplined Entrepreneurship framework is fully explained in two books:

1) Disciplined Entrepreneurship: 24 Steps to a Successful Startup by Bill Aulet

2) Disciplined Entrepreneurship Workbook by Bill Aulet

 

Second: You can find more resources on the d-eship.com site

 

Lastly: MIT Bootcamps has produced 3 MOOCs covering the first three themes of the Disciplined Entrepreneurship Framework. You can view archived courses here

https://www.d-eship.com/resources-2/courses/

 

The videos from this online course may be found on the MIT Bootcamps Youtube Channel.
