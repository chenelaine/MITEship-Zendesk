CATEGORY: MIT Specific questions
SECTION: Navigating startup resources at MIT

If you are an enrolled student, you can visit Orbit, the one stop shop for MIT student entrepreneurs, and click "Contact an EIR" to post your request. An EIR can get back to you on resources that other MIT students have curated.

We do not post this information on line because this is a dynamic list, and also because we do not endorse any service provider.
