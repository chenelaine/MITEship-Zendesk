Are there on line resources to help me learn entrepreneurship?


CATEGORY: General questions
SECTION: Getting started

Yes! There are lots of free online resources to help you learn entrepreneurship at your own pace.

For on-line entrepreneurship education, there are the three Entrepreneurship MOOCs by Bill and 1 User Innovation MOOC by Eric von Hippel

https://www.d-eship.com/resources-2/courses/

MIT Bootcamps also has an e-seminar series featuring real entrepreneurs.

https://orbit-kb.mit.edu/hc/en-us/articles/360024372532-E-Seminars-from-MIT-Bootcamps

Y-Combinator’s Startup School has a lot of archived content that are fabulous

https://www.startupschool.org/videos

The Stanford eCorner is also a great resource with a lot of great videos, podcasts articles and the like.

https://ecorner.stanford.edu/
