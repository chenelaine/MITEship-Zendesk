What is the difference between a utility patent and a design patent?


CATEGORY: Legal
SECTION: Intellectual Property Protection
  • A full-on patent application for a US utility patent covers the actual invention of a system or method to do something novel. It includes the following distinct sections:
    • Description
    • Diagrams
    • References depicting a prior art search
    • A set of claims
  • A design patent protects the esthetic design of a device, and nothing else. Â Design patents are fully defined with line drawings depicting the device with 7 views: 3D/isometric, top, front, left, right, bottom, back.
