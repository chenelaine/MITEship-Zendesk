Customer support and ticketing: Zendesk for Startups


CATEGORY: Free or discounted services for startups
SECTION: Business systems

Zendesk provides a customer support and engagement ticketing platform.

Zendesk for Startups is a special program that provides a monthly credit for six (6) months of up to one hundred (100) Agents redeemable for any combination of Zendesk Support, Talk, Chat, Guide, Explore, and Sell products, for free. Consulting Services, add-ons, integrations, and Zendesk Talk or Zendesk Sell minutes are not included.

To take advantage of this perk you should apply here. Please note that this offer is available only to a new Zendesk customer, Series A or below, and have less than 50 employees. Learn more here.

This perk was last verified in February, 2020.
