What are some options for commercial accelerators for startups?


CATEGORY: Raising money - Grants, prizes and accelerators
SECTION: Accelerators

Crunchbase maintains a list of top accelerators around the world. Check it out.

https://about.crunchbase.com/blog/100-startup-accelerators-around-the-world/

Mass Technology Challenge maintains a list of incubators and accelerators in the greater Boston area. Check it out: 

https://masstech.org/why-massachusetts/other-technology-resources/incubators-and-accelerators

Starthub Boston also maintains a similar list.

https://www.starthub.org/incubators

We have also heard about some accelerators for student entrepreneurs, some of which are backed by Venture Capital (VC) firms. While these are great resources for students, know that you may run into a signaling issue if you go through an accelerator backed by a specific VC, and then you end up not raising money from them. This could be perceived as a flag by other potential investors.
