CATEGORY: Free or discounted services for startups
SECTION: General / Overview

There are a lot of free or discounted perks available to help early stage startups get going. Many of those perks are temporary - but 3 months free is 3 months free.

This section was last updated on February 1, 2020. These perks continuously change and evolve. We encourage you to have a look at the perks in this section, and do your own research to see what resources like those listed here might apply to you. 

Please note that these resources are provided for your reference only. MIT does not endorse any one of these vendors. Please do your own research and adopt the tool that best meets your needs.
