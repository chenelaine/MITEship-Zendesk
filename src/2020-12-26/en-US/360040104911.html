What goes in an innovation strategy?


CATEGORY: Corporate Entrepreneurship
SECTION: Innovation Strategy in Corporations

Following is an example table of contents from the approved innovation strategy of a major multi-national non-profit organization.

  • Introduction
  • Purpose
  • Objective of the Innovation Strategy
  • Barriers to Innovation and Proposed Solutions
  • Approach to Implementing the Innovation Strategy
  • Roadmap
  • Implementation Plan for the current fiscal year
  • The Innovation Unit's Role in Supporting the Delivery of the Strategy
  • Resourcing
  • Measuring Impact
  • Appendix 1. An overview of the accelerator program
  • Appendix 2. iLab Activity - Workshops and special events
