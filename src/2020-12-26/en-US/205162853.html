I am new to entrepreneurship. What are some good books to read / blogs to follow?


CATEGORY: General questions
SECTION: Getting started

Here is a suggested reading list. Enjoy.

Good reads on Strategy and Leadership

  • Harvard Business Review is a great source of wisdom.
  • Linda Hill and Maria Farkas have a great HBR article titled "Note on Team Process" that helps leaders build and nurture teams.
  • Hubspot has open-sourced their Culture Code that explains how they create a "lovable company".
  • Zappos shares their Culture Book which contains unedited submissions from their own employees.

Blogs 

  • Y-Combinator News. 
  • SaaStr (for SaaS businesses)
  • QuickSprout
  • Seth's Blog
  • Joel.is
  • Jeff Bussgang - Flybridge Partners. "Seeing both sides"
  • David Beisel, Rob Go et al. Next View Ventures blog.
  • Ben Einstein - BoltVC. "Musings on the intersection of hardware and startups"
  • Scott Miller et al - Dragon Innovation. "Dragon Innovation Blog"
  • Dharmesh Shah - Hubspot. "On Startups"
  • David Skok - Matrix Partners. "For entrepreneurs"
  • Mark Suster - Upfront Ventures. "Both sides of the table"

Podcasts

  • Reid Hoffman's "Masters of Scale"
  • NPR's "How I built this"

MOOCs

  • Disciplined Entrepreneurship series
    • Entrepreneurship 101: "Who is your customer?"  
    • Entrepreneurship 102: "What can you do for your customer?" 
    • Entrepreneurship 103: "Show me the money!"
  • "User centered innovation: A pathway to entrepreneurship" by MIT Professor Eric Von Hippel
  • "Becoming an Entrepreneur" by Laurie Stach
  • Startup School - YCombinator
  • "Launch your startup" - Columbia Business School
