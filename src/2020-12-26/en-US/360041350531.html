What services and support can I get from the Martin Trust Center if I am not a student?


CATEGORY: MIT Specific questions
SECTION: Martin Trust Center for MIT Entrepreneurship

Martin Trust Center has a lot of services that are focused on current MIT students only. However, there are resources that are available to non students (including but not limited to MIT alumni, staff and other affliates). Following is a short list:

  • On-line educational resources for learning entrepreneurship frameworks, tools and practices:  See this knowledgebase article
  • Orbit: Events and deadlines
  • Orbit: Jobs (posting jobs)
  • Orbit: Startups (list of MIT delta v alumni teams 2012-present)
  • Orbit: Investments (list of which MIT delta v alumni teams raised money to date)
  • Orbit: Knowledgebase (620 articles counting!)
  • MTC: Newsletter 

MIT affiliates who are not current students, who are in the greater Boston area can also engage with the Venture Mentoring Service (http://vms.mit.edu).
